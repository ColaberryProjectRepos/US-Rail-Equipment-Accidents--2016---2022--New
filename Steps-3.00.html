Executive Summary

              

Challenges Encountered:

1.Choosing the fields to use in my dataset. My dataset contained 65 columns of which 80% were important for this report, so it was hard to decide which one to leave. I decided to focus on the fields that were most directly related to the project title.

2.Fixing the map visualization. At first, I used a shape map, but I could not drill down then I changed to global map. The global map gave me an error, I opened preview features in the settings to enable the map and filled map, but it was not available in the list. This took me time to figure out but finally I found out that the map and filled map were disabled in my power bi service. I logged in to the power bi service and enabled them.

3.Hiding 0 values on KPIs and Indicators. It took me time to figure out how to hide the KPIs and indicators when the value is zero. I had to use conditional color formatting to achieve it.

4.Using ShareX. I had challenges recording GIFs that met the required size limit for the project, so I kept repeating for so many times. I solved this challenge by increasing my clicking speed, setting the drilldowns on before recording and focusing on the most important clicks only.
