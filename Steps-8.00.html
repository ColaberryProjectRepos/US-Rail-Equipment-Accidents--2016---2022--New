Download and Load CSV File to Power BI Desktop 8

  1. Download Rail Equipment Accident/Incident data from DATA.GOV and save the data file on your device, or download the data from this icon  on top of this step and save it on your device.
  2. Open Power BI Desktop and click on GetData then under GetData select Text,CVS, Connect.  Then select the saved data File. open it.
  3. Click on transform data.
