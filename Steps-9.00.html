  1. Remove other columns that are not needed for this report . Select all the required columns(Accident Number, Accident Year, Accident Month, Date, Station, State Name, Equipment Type, Total Damage Cost, Accident Cause, Total Persons Killed and Total Persons Injured), right click and select Remove other columns.
  2. Change accident date type  to Date, and Accident year to whole number. Right click on Date column, select Change Type then select Date. Right click on Accident year column, select Change Type then select Whole Number.
  3. Filter accident year to remain with years between 2016 and 2022. Click on the accident year drop down, click number filters, select between , then type/select 16 and 22.
